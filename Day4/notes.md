#Operators in Python

1. Arithmetic Operators:
->Addition - (+)
->Subtraction - (-)
->Multiplication - (*)
->Division - (/)
->Modulus - (%)
->Floor Division - (//)
->Exponent - (**)

2. Comparison Operator:
->Equal - (==)
->Not equal - (!=)
->Greater than - (>)

->Greater than or equal - (>=)
->Less than or equal - (<=)

3. Logical Operators:
->and - (&)
->or - (|)
->not - (!)

4. Assignment Operators:
-> =
-> +
-> -=

-> /=
